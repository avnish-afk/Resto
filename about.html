About Resto

Welcome to Resto

Welcome to Resto, your trusted partner in restaurant billing management. We specialize in helping restaurants streamline their billing processes, making it easier than ever to deliver bills directly to customers' phones via text message.

At Resto, we believe that efficiency and customer satisfaction go hand in hand. Our innovative platform simplifies the billing experience and enhances communication between restaurants and their patrons, ensuring a seamless dining experience.

Join us in revolutionizing the restaurant industry. With Resto, you can focus on what you do best—serving delicious food and providing exceptional service—while we take care of the billing.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower restaurants with cutting-edge technology that boosts operational efficiency and enhances customer satisfaction. We strive to be the backbone of your billing process, allowing you to create memorable experiences for your guests.
